Common Situations Where Contractors File Liens

💰

Unpaid Invoice by Owner

The property owner refuses to pay the final invoice despite completed work.

⚖

Dispute Over Payment

The property owner or general contractor contests the amount owed for work performed.

📄

Change Order Conflicts

Disagreements arise over additional work or modifications to the contract.

⏳

Delayed or Withheld Payment

The property owner repeatedly delays payment even after multiple requests.
